## Creating a New Scenario

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Scenarios">
    Click on "Sc√©narios" in the left sidebar menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New">
    Click the "+" button to create a new scenario
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Basic Configuration">
    Configure:
    - Scenario name
    - Description (important for Natalia's understanding)
    - Type of interaction
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important Notes

<Note>
The description you provide in your scenario is automatically added to Natalia's training. Be specific and clear about:
- The purpose of the scenario
- Expected interactions
- Desired outcomes
</Note>

## Linking Connectors

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Connectors">
    Open your scenario settings and navigate to the Connectors section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Connectors">
    Choose the connectors needed for your scenario:
    - REST API for custom integrations
    - Call Transfer for human handoff
    - More connectors coming soon
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set up each connector's specific settings
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Testing Your Scenario

<Steps>
  <Step title="Development Mode">
    Test your scenario in development mode first
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Responses">
    Check that Natalia responds correctly to various inputs
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Once verified, deploy to production
  </Step>
</Steps>
