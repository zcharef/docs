## Best Practices

### Call Management
- Configure business hours accurately
- Set up appropriate greetings
- Define clear routing rules
- Establish escalation procedures

### Scenario Creation
- Start with simple scenarios
- Test thoroughly before deployment
- Monitor and adjust based on feedback
- Gradually add complexity

### Customer Experience
- Keep messages clear and concise
- Maintain a professional tone
- Ensure smooth handoffs to human agents
- Regular scenario updates

### Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Can Natalia adapt to specific industries?">
    Absolutely. Natalia adapts to your business domain through dashboard configurations: company presentation, sales/welcome scripts, and sector-specific information for more relevant responses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="question" title="How does Natalia handle specific or unusual questions?">
    Natalia knows her limits. When a request is outside her expertise, she smoothly transfers the conversation to a human team member, ensuring optimal customer care.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="users" title="Does Natalia replace human teams?">
    No, Natalia is designed to support, not replace your teams. She handles repetitive tasks while allowing your staff to focus on complex requests.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="microphone" title="Can we customize Natalia's voice and tone?">
    While voice customization is in development, Natalia maintains a professional and warm tone that represents your company well.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
