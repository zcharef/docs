## Setup Your Natalia Assistant

Learn how to set up and configure your Natalia AI assistant in minutes.

### Initial Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    1. Visit [app.getnatalia.com](https://app.getnatalia.com)
    2. Click on "Sign Up"
    3. Fill in your business information
    4. Verify your email address
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Basic Configuration">
    1. Log into your dashboard
    2. Configure company information
    3. Define routing rules
    4. Set up your scenarios
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Communication">
    1. Set up phone numbers
    2. Configure email integration
    3. Test your setup
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Scenario

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Scenarios">
    Navigate to the Scenarios section in your dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Type">
    Select from:
    - Call handling
    - Email management
    - Custom workflow
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set up:
    - Greeting messages
    - Routing rules
    - Response templates
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test and Deploy">
    1. Test thoroughly
    2. Make adjustments
    3. Deploy to production
  </Step>
</Steps>
