## Getting Started with Natalia

Transform your customer service with Natalia in three simple steps. No technical expertise required.

### Initial Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Create Your Account">
    1. Visit [app.getnatalia.com](https://app.getnatalia.com)
    2. Click on "Sign Up"
    3. Fill in your business information
    4. Verify your email address
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="gear" title="Basic Configuration">
    1. Log into your dashboard
    2. Set your business hours
       - Define your typical weekly schedule
       - Customize hours for specific days or events
    3. Configure company information
    4. Define routing rules
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="phone" title="Configure Communication Channels">
    1. Set up phone numbers
    2. Configure email integration
    3. Test your setup
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Creating Your First Scenario

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Scenarios">
    Navigate to the Scenarios section in your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New">
    Click "Create New Scenario"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Type">
    Select from:
    - Call handling
    - Email management
    - Custom workflow
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set up:
    - Greeting messages
    - Routing rules
    - Response templates
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test">
    Test your scenario thoroughly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Deploy to production
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

### Call Management
- Configure business hours accurately
- Set up appropriate greetings
- Define clear routing rules
- Establish escalation procedures

### Scenario Creation
- Start with simple scenarios
- Test thoroughly before deployment
- Monitor and adjust based on feedback
- Gradually add complexity

### Customer Experience
- Keep messages clear and concise
- Maintain a professional tone
- Ensure smooth handoffs to human agents
- Regular scenario updates

### Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Can Natalia adapt to specific industries?">
    Absolutely. Natalia adapts to your business domain through dashboard configurations: company presentation, sales/welcome scripts, and sector-specific information for more relevant responses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="question" title="How does Natalia handle specific or unusual questions?">
    Natalia knows her limits. When a request is outside her expertise, she smoothly transfers the conversation to a human team member, ensuring optimal customer care.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="users" title="Does Natalia replace human teams?">
    No, Natalia is designed to support, not replace your teams. She handles repetitive tasks while allowing your staff to focus on complex requests.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="microphone" title="Can we customize Natalia's voice and tone?">
    While voice customization is in development, Natalia maintains a professional and warm tone that represents your company well.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
