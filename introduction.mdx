## Core Features

### Call Management
Natalia handles recurring calls and frequently asked questions instantly, using your real-time data for queries about opening hours or order tracking.

### Custom Scenarios
Configure Natalia to handle specific requests, such as providing product information or managing key steps in your internal processes.

### Intelligent Routing
When a request becomes complex, Natalia knows when to hand over to a team member, transferring all necessary information for quick handling.
