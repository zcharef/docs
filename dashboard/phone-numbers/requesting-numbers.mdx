## Getting New Numbers

Currently, phone numbers cannot be automatically ordered through the dashboard. To obtain a new phone number or if you have any questions, please contact our support team.

### How to Request

1. Email support@getnatalia.com
2. Include your account details
3. Specify your requirements:
   - Number of lines needed
   - Geographic preferences
   - Use case description

## After Getting Your Number

Once you receive your new number:
1. Configure it in your dashboard
2. Link it to appropriate scenarios
3. Set up business hours (coming soon)
4. Test thoroughly before going live
